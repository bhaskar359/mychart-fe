Instructions

You can use Aquaphor over the wound to keep it moist while it heals. This is an over-the-counter ointment and can be applied lightly 2–3 times daily.

Wound Care

  • Clean the wound gently with mild soap and water.
  • Avoid scrubbing or applying pressure to the injured area.
  • Apply Aquaphor to maintain a moist healing environment.
  • Keep the area covered if there's a risk of friction or dirt.
  • Do not use alcohol, hydrogen peroxide, or harsh antiseptics.

Pain & Discomfort

Mild discomfort is normal. You may use:

  • Acetaminophen (Tylenol) for pain relief.
  • Ibuprofen (Advil/Motrin) to reduce inflammation.

Follow recommended dosing on the package unless advised otherwise by your provider.

When to Seek Immediate Care

  • Increasing redness, swelling, or warmth around the wound.
  • Yellow or green discharge.
  • Fever above 100.4°F (38°C).
  • Severe pain or rapid worsening of symptoms.
  • Red streaking spreading from the wound.

Follow-Up Instructions

No scheduled follow-ups are required unless symptoms worsen. Please contact USF Student Health Services if you have concerns or notice signs of infection.
